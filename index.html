COMPUTER VISION & COMPUTER GRAPHICS

Better Visuals for Minecraft

Custom Minecraft Shader Program, May 10th, 2022

Implemented an efficient shader that gives decent shadows to the game. Minecraft is a classic game, but the default visuals are pretty basic and unrealistic. The goal of this project is to implement a shader program that provides higher graphics fidelity, in the hope of enhancing minecraft player’s gaming experience.

Cloth Simulation

Visual simulation of Cloth, April 29th, 2022

Implemented visual simulation of cloth using the classic model of point masses and strings, along with a basic shader program that renders a realistic fabric outlook. The simulation I present also involves cloth collisions with other objects (spheres and plane), and collisions with itself.

Pathtracer

Rendering 3D scenes April 12, 2022

Rendering of 3D scenes, implemented both direct illumination and global illumination. Process includes generating sample rays from camera to objects in the scene, efficiently detecting ray-object intersections using Bounding Volume Hierarchy, Monte Carlo Estimator to simulate realistic lightings on diffuse surfaces. Lastly, I applied adaptive sampling scheme to optimize the performance of the sampling procedure.

Mesh Editing

Rendering bezier curves and 3d meshes, March 30th, 2022

Geometric modeling techniques raphics of rendering objects in 1d, 2d and 3d space, drawing bezier curves and surfaces, creating fine shadings for 3d meshes that mimics real world objects, flipping and splitting of mesh edges that aids the operation of mesh upsampling to achieve better display and more accurate simulation.

Rasterizer

Rendering images on screen, March 12, 2022

Implemented the Rasterization of points, lines and triangles, along with various advanced methods of samplings which greatly suppressed unwanted visual effects and improved the quality of the rasterization.

Image Filters

Filters and Frequency, October, 2020

Implemented various techniques of images filters, including edge detection, image blurring (gaussian filters), straightening, sharpening, creating hybrid images, multiresolution blending using gaussian stack and laplacian stack.

Face Morphing

face morphing works, October, 2020

Face morphing is simultaneous warp of the image shape and a cross-dissolve of the image colors. Aside from creating face morphing of celebrities, I also computed the average face of an ethnicity population.

Facial Keypoint Detection with Neural Networks

Nosetips and facial keypoints detection, November, 2020

Used neural networks to automatically detect facial keypoints. Work includes detecting the coordinates of people's nosetips in images, as well as predicting 58 facial keypoints in faces. The artifitial neural network is trained on a large dataset of IMM Face Database

AutoStitching Photo Mosaics

AutoStitching Photo Mosaics, December, 2020

This project is about stitching together many photographs to create larger composite images, including image warping and mosaicing.
